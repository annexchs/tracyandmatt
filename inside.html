Our Story

If anyone was ever going to ask Matt or Tracy how they thought they would meet their future spouses they would have laughed if you told them they would meet online!

Tracy had recently moved to Charleston and thought that trying online dating might be a good way to meet new people in town, little did she know she would meet her husband!

The first day of being on the website, Tracy found Matt and was instantly drawn to his amazing smile. Tracy messaged Matt and he was hooked! Just a few messages later and Matt asked Tracy out for drinks. To say the connection was instantaneous would be an understatement.

They stayed at the bar talking for hours, feeling like they had known each other for years. If it wasn’t for the bar closing they probably would have been there talking all weekend! Within just a few weeks Tracy had met Matt’s parents, Matt’s mom had casually in conversation slipped that Matt was in love with Tracy, and the couple had an unforgettable weekend in Florida visiting with Tracy’s parents. From the day they met they knew that the search was over, the love story of Matt and Tracy had officially begun!
